The test will focus on the following contents:

  • Number representation;
  • Errors and error propagation;
  • Roots of real functions:
    • root isolation,
    • interval methods - bissection and false position,
    • initial guess,
    • non interval methods -Newton and Picard-Peano,
    • convergence,
    • closing /  stoping,
    • implementation eficiency.
  • Systems of non linear equations:  Newton and Picard-Peano;
  • Systems of linear equations:
    • Direct methods (Gauss elimination, Cholesky or Kaletsky decomposition);
    • Errors  in Gaussian elimination: internal and external sensitivity analysis;
    • Iterative methods (Gauss-Jacobi and Gauss-Seidel);
    • Error analysis in linear systems;  
  • Quadrature and cubature:
    • Trapezoidal rule;
    • Simpson's rule;
    • Choice of integration step, the ratio of convergence and the error estimation;
    • Extension to the double integration.
  • Equations and systems of ordinary differential equations:
    • Integration by Euler method;
    • Integration by Runge-Kutta methods;
    • Choice of integration step, the ratio of convergence and the error estimation.
  • Unconstrained non linear optimization:
    • Single dimension:
      • Golden search algorithm;
    • Multidimensional:
      • Gradient method;
      • Quadratic method;
      • Levenberg-Marquardt algorithm.
